P 5150: 1. LAW
P 5151: No modern society functions without local governing bodies, elected by some or all of the citizens living in the place. Switzerland is unique among developed countries in refusing to allow women to vote. Differences arise mainly in the amount of power or initiative allowed by law or practice to any local body. In America the rights of the fifty separated states against the Federal Government are guaranteed in the constitution. Inside the states, cities and other communities govern themselves to a large extent. How much freedom can be allowed? How much centralisation of government should there be? How should a country be run efficiently without limiting too much the rights of the individual? These are questions yet not entirely answered even by experts on government and constitutional law and administration.
